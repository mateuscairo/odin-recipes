Moqueca de Peixe

Description

Moqueca de peixe is one of the most famous foods in Bahia-Brazil. Always getting together a fresh fish, a local ingrediente: "dendê" and the flavour of bell pepper.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of fresh fish (700g)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 big onion sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper sliced
  • 2 mature tomatoes sliced
  • 2 soup spoons of cilantro
  • 200ml of coconut milk
  • 1 soup spoon of "dendê" oil
  • 2 shrimps broth tablet

Steps

  1. Get the fish cleaned, drizzle with the lemon juice and let it rest for 1 hour
  2. In a big pan, put the fish, the onions, the bell peppers, the tomatoes and cilantro
  3. Mix the shrimp broth tablet with the coconut milk and drizzle the fish
  4. With the pan half capped, take to low heat for 20 minutes
  5. Stir the pan a few times until it get cooked
  6. Put the "dendê" oil and salt
  7. Remove from heat and serve
